# Semiconductor and IC Manufacturers

This document provides a broad working list of active semiconductor manufacturers and semiconductor brands. It includes conventional integrated circuits and specialty devices such as:

- RF and microwave ICs
- PIN, Schottky, varactor, limiter, detector, and step-recovery diodes
- RF switches, attenuators, mixers, and phase shifters
- MMIC and monolithic amplifiers
- BJTs, JFETs, MOSFETs, IGBTs, GaN, and SiC devices
- Power-management, analog, digital, memory, sensor, and optoelectronic ICs

> Note: This is not literally every semiconductor company worldwide. Many small regional, custom-ASIC, foundry-only, and military suppliers also exist.

## Replacement-Part Guidance

For replacement-component work:

- Treat former brands separately from active manufacturers.
- Prefer currently active, supported manufacturer part numbers.
- Do not recommend discontinued or end-of-life parts as long-term replacements.
- Label obsolete parts clearly when they are included only as temporary procurement bridges.
